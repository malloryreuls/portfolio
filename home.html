Multimedia Wikipedia

     Multimedia Wikipedia (AskDMX) is a multimedia encyclopedia leveraging three API's: Freebase (for Wikipedia description), YouTube, and Google Images. Multimedia Wikipedia is a one-stop shop for learning more about any Wikipedia topic. MMW also has a custom analytics dashboard that tracks the last ten searches that have been made in the app and most frequent searches that have been made over time. MMW's stand out feature is the full screen search bar, which is triggered by any key event.

Technologies:
